Food Philosophy

Herbs Are the Heart of the Experience

At Relic & Graze, herbs are the heart of the experience.

We begin with what is fresh, seasonal, and locally sourced, allowing the ingredients to guide the creation of each recipe. Rather than fitting nature into a menu, we build our offerings around what is growing, thriving, and at its most vibrant.

From garden-inspired flavors to handcrafted recipes, every element is thoughtfully designed to celebrate the beauty, simplicity, and nourishment found in real ingredients.

Signature Experiences

Five Signature Experiences

A sensory journey through wonder, water, earth, connection, and nourishment.

Each experience invites guests to discover something, receive something, create something, or carry something home.

Wandering Altar Objects, Stones & Candles
01 Wandering Altar

Maybe It Chose You

A discovery altar filled with meaningful objects, natural elements, and small curiosities.

Guests are invited to wander, pause, and connect with whatever calls to them. Each item carries a feeling, story, or intention.

Purpose

A moment of curiosity, reflection, and personal connection.

A Drop of Eden Water, Glass & Light
02 A Drop of Eden

Living Water Ritual

A shared water blessing experience centered around the beauty and symbolism of living water.

Guests may offer:

  • A blessing
  • A wish
  • Gratitude
  • An intention

The water becomes a symbolic collection of the gathering’s love, gratitude, and shared energy. Guests are invited to take home a tiny glass vial as a meaningful keepsake.

Vial Tag

Blessed Water — a sacred drop to carry with you.

Purpose

To carry a piece of the gathering home.

Integration Salt Botanical Salt Blending
03 Integration Salt

The Apothecary Ritual

A botanical blending experience inspired by herbs, flowers, and earth elements.

Guests create their own take-home offering using:

  • Mineral salts
  • Dried flowers
  • Herbs
  • Essential oils
  • Botanical elements

The final creation may be packaged in beautiful envelopes or tins.

Purpose

A sensory ritual of grounding, integration, and remembrance.

Love, Someone Handwritten Love Notes
04 Love, Someone

Leave love. Receive love. Love, someone.

A handwritten exchange of kindness, connection, and intention.

Guests are invited to:

  • Write a message, blessing, mantra, or words of love
  • Leave one behind
  • Receive one from another

The handmade envelope installation becomes part of the experience itself.

Purpose

A quiet exchange of love and connection between strangers, families, and loved ones.
